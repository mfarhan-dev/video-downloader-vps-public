GET /extract?url=<video_url>
Extract video metadata and available formats. Returns title, thumbnail, duration, and a list of streamable formats. 
curl -s "https://api.srv1053900.hstgr.cloud/extract?url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ" | python3 -m json.tool
ParamTypeRequiredDescription
urlstringYesVideo URL (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.)

GET /download?url=<video_url>&format_id=<id>
Proxy-download a specific video format. Re-extracts a fresh stream URL and pipes the binary back to you. 
curl -o video.mp4 "https://api.srv1053900.hstgr.cloud/download?url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ&format_id=136"
ParamTypeRequiredDescription
urlstringYesSame video URL used for /extract
format_idstringYesFormat ID from /extract response
